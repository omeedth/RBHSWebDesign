Web Design

Web design is a compilation of skills which combined creates a professional website like this one!

Web Design Skills

  1. Conceptualizing and planning the layout of the website including the color schemes and font
  2. Creating graphic designs such as logos
  3. Coding the website using various languages such as HTML5, CSS3, JQuery, etc.

About Us

Founder

I am Alex Thomas the founder of the Rancho Bernardo High School Web Design club and the creator of the website. I am in the class of 2017, and through my interest in computer science I have taught myself how to develop websites. With this newfound skill I hope to spread my knowledge to other students interested in computer science or web development!

What You Will Learn

Web Development is a combination of 3 different programming languages

  1. 1. HTML5 - the skeleton of the website         
  2. 2. CSS3 - the color and design of the website
  3. 3. JQuery - the interactivity of the website     

Why Is This Important

Computer science is a rapidly expanding field which has much to offer to people interested in it. Programmers are in high demand in our society filled with technology. The internet is a vast pool of information and opportunity waiting to be tapped into. This club offers an introduction to computer science as well as sharpen your skills in web development necessary for our tech savvy world!

What We Do

The club will be pursuing many web design projects where the students will be able to foster their
creativity with their own designs and website layouts. The president will be the instructor of
the club, teaching the students HTML5, CSS3, and JQuery then allow the students to exhibit their skills.
